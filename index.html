My Design Process

  1. Discover & Research: Interviews and analysis to uncover user needs and challenges.
  2. Problem & Scope: Define goals, personas, and project roadmap.
  3. Ideate & Sketch: Creative brainstorming and initial sketches.
  4. Design & Prototype: Interactive or tangible prototypes for testing.
  5. Test & Iterate: Refinement based on feedback and usability testing.
  6. Handover & Monitor: Developer collaboration and ongoing improvements.
